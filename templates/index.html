Welcome to MSTPit!

What's MSTPit?

MSTPit stands for Medical Scientist Training Program Information Transfer, or alternatively for Medical Scientist Training Pit. The goal of this website is to serve as a central place for the student body to communicate with each other and the office easily.

How to use the site?

To start using this site, login using the login window to the right! If you have not received your login information yet, please contact the office!
